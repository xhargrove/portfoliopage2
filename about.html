Air Force/Veteran

Straight out of high school, I made the decision to enlist in the Air Force, eager to serve my country and seek new challenges. The choice to join the military was driven by a desire to step outside my comfort zone, develop discipline, and gain valuable skills that would set the foundation for my future. The experience was transformative, teaching me resilience, teamwork, and the importance of dedication. It wasn't just about learning to follow orders—it was about becoming a leader, developing a strong work ethic, and understanding the true meaning of commitment. The camaraderie and structure provided by the Air Force helped shape me into the person I am today, instilling a sense of pride and responsibility that I carry with me in all my endeavors.

Being part of the Air Force straight out of high school was a life-changing experience that opened up a world of opportunities. The rigorous training and diverse challenges pushed me to grow both mentally and physically. I had the chance to travel, work with cutting-edge technology, and collaborate with individuals from all walks of life, each with a unique background and set of skills. This journey not only gave me a clear sense of purpose but also a strong appreciation for the importance of hard work and perseverance. The Air Force became more than just a career choice—it was a path that allowed me to mature quickly, take on responsibilities, and build a solid foundation for everything I would pursue in the future.

Business Owner

DJ Big X, also known as Spearhead X, is a seasoned and highly respected figure in the music industry, with a career that spans decades and countless contributions to hip-hop and urban music culture. Known for his deep passion for music and dynamic mixing skills, DJ Big X has worked with numerous influential artists and played a crucial role in promoting independent music through various platforms and showcases. As a founding member of the Coalition DJs, he has helped shape the Atlanta music scene, connecting artists, DJs, and industry professionals and creating spaces where emerging talent can shine. His dedication to supporting and uplifting independent artists has earned him a reputation as a true industry pioneer and tastemaker. Beyond the turntables, DJ Big X has built a legacy through mentorship and visionary projects. He continuously explores new ways to innovate and impact the music industry, from curating events to producing content that resonates with both artists and fans. His recent projects, like “New Music Mondays,” provide invaluable exposure for up-and-coming artists, blending performance with professional feedback to create a platform for artistic growth.
